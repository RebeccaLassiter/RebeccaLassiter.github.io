Hello there! I am a sophomore studying Computer Science at Northwestern University. I am currently interested in Tangible Interaction Design and Embodied Computing--exploring how we can create user interfaces that utilize greater physical or full-body interaction. If you want to learn more about me and my work, check out my Projects page and the Miscellaneous Interests below!
