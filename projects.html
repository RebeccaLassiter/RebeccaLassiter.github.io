I worked with a team to conduct analysis about disaster preparedness. Using our research, I created this policy tool prototype. Community leaders can enter certain characteristics of their municipality (average income, number of physicians, etc) and they will be given data visualizations and relevant policy suggestions to become more resilient to natural disasters. Red Cross Hackathon

I worked on an interdisciplinary team to hypothesize the reason for a decrease in Red Cross fire incident responses. We interviewed Red Cross officials, risk management experts, and fire marshals to inform analysis decisions. I used Red Cross incident response data, American Community Survey data, and various data sets from the Chicago Open Data Portal to engage in exploratory data analysis and data visualization using GIS and R. We presented research and findings to a panel of Red Cross officials, data scientists, and other experts in the field. Take a look at our results.

Transit Ridership Analysis

I used data from the Chicago Open Data portal to analyze the impact of rideshares on Chicago public transit ridership. I analyzed various stakeholders, policy implications, and the impacts of accessibility and income inequality on ridership. I conducted exploratory data analysis and visualization with matplotlib and GIS I also used R to carry out a time series analysis to analyze ridership trends over time. Check out my findings.
